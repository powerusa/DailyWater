# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-02-08

This Privacy Policy explains how **DailyWater** ("the App") handles information.

## Summary
- The App does **not** collect, store, or share personal data on a server.
- Water intake data is stored **locally on your device** only.
- The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or trackers.

## Information We Collect
The App does not collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or identifiers.

### Water Intake Data
If you enter water intake amounts, goals, and units, this information is stored **only on your device** to make the App work.

## How Your Data Is Used
Your locally stored data is used only to:
- Track today's water intake
- Show progress toward your daily goal
- Reset daily totals when the date changes

## Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services
The App does not use third-party analytics or advertising services.

## Security
Your data remains on your device. You control it and can delete it by deleting the App (or using any reset feature inside the App).

## Children's Privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted at this same URL.
